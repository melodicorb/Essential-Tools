About Quiz Creator

The Quiz Creator is a versatile tool that helps you create, edit, and share custom quizzes. This tool is particularly useful for:

  • Teachers creating assessments for their students
  • Trainers developing knowledge checks for workshops
  • Students creating study materials for exams
  • Content creators making interactive quizzes for their audience
  • Anyone looking to test knowledge on specific topics

Our tool offers multiple question types, customizable options, and the ability to save and share your quizzes. Simply select your desired mode, create or select a quiz, and get started!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a quiz?

To create a quiz, select "Create New Quiz" from the Quiz Mode dropdown. Enter a title and description for your quiz, then add questions using the "Add Question" button. For each question, you can select the question type and add options. Once you're done, click "Save Quiz" to store your quiz.

What question types are supported?

The Quiz Creator supports multiple question types: Multiple Choice (select one correct answer from several options), True/False (binary choice questions), and Short Answer (free text responses that can be evaluated against expected answers).

How are quizzes saved?

Quizzes are saved locally in your browser's storage. This means they will persist between sessions on the same device and browser, but won't be accessible from other devices unless you share them.

How do I share my quiz with others?

After saving your quiz, you'll see a "Share Quiz" section with a unique link. Copy this link and share it with others. When they open the link, they'll be able to take your quiz directly.
