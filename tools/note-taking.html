Note Taking Tool

Create, organize, and manage your notes with our simple and effective note-taking application.

Features:

  • Create and edit notes with rich text formatting
  • Organize notes with categories
  • Search through your notes
  • Auto-save functionality
  • Local storage for privacy

How to Use

  1. Click "New Note" to create a new note
  2. Give your note a title
  3. Write your note content in the editor
  4. Select a category for your note (optional)
  5. Your notes are automatically saved
  6. Use the search bar to find specific notes
