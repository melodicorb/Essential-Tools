About Habit Tracker

This habit tracker tool helps you build and maintain positive habits by tracking your daily activities. Research shows it takes about 66 days to form a new habit - our tool makes it easy to stay consistent and visualize your progress.

How to Use

  • Add a new habit using the form at the top
  • Set the frequency (daily, weekly, or custom days)
  • Check off your habits when you complete them
  • View your streaks and statistics to stay motivated
  • Set reminders to help you remember your habits

Features

  • Track multiple habits with different frequencies
  • Visualize your progress with streak counters
  • Set custom reminder notifications
  • View statistics on your habit completion rate
  • Customizable interface with theme options

FAQ

How long does it take to form a habit?

Research suggests it takes anywhere from 21 to 66 days to form a new habit, depending on the complexity of the habit and individual differences.

What happens if I miss a day?

Missing a day will reset your current streak, but your overall statistics will still track your progress. Don't get discouraged - consistency over time is what matters most!

Can I export my habit data?

Currently, all data is stored locally in your browser. We're working on an export feature for a future update.
