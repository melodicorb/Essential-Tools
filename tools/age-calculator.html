About Age Calculator

The Age Calculator computes your exact age in years, months, and days based on your birth date. It also provides additional information such as your zodiac sign, day of birth, and days until your next birthday.

How to Use

  1. Enter your birth date (day, month, and year)
  2. Optionally, specify a reference date other than today
  3. Click the "Calculate Age" button
  4. View your exact age and additional information

Features

  • Precise age calculation in years, months, and days
  • Total age in various units (months, weeks, days, hours)
  • Day of the week you were born
  • Zodiac sign and Chinese zodiac animal
  • Next birthday countdown
  • Option to calculate age between any two dates

FAQ

How is age calculated?

Age is calculated by finding the difference between the birth date and reference date. The calculator accounts for varying month lengths and leap years to provide an accurate result.

Why might my age calculation differ from other calculators?

Different calculators may use slightly different methods for handling partial months. This calculator uses the most common approach of counting complete months and remaining days.

Is my data stored when I use this calculator?

No, all calculations are performed locally in your browser. No birth date or personal information is transmitted or stored on our servers.
