About This Tool

Our To-Do List Manager helps you organize your tasks and boost productivity. With this simple yet powerful tool, you can:

  • Create and manage your daily tasks
  • Mark tasks as completed
  • Delete tasks you no longer need
  • Clear all completed tasks at once
  • Keep track of remaining tasks

The tool uses local storage to save your tasks, so they'll still be there when you return to the page later (as long as you use the same device and browser).

How to Use

  1. Add a task: Type your task in the input field and click the "Add Task" button or press Enter.
  2. Mark as completed: Click the checkbox next to a task to mark it as completed. Completed tasks will be displayed with a strikethrough.
  3. Delete a task: Click the trash icon next to a task to remove it from your list.
  4. Clear completed tasks: Click the "Clear completed tasks" button to remove all tasks that have been marked as completed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my tasks saved if I close the browser?

Yes, your tasks are saved in your browser's local storage. They will persist even if you close the browser or restart your computer, as long as you use the same device and browser and don't clear your browser data.

Can I access my tasks from another device?

No, tasks are stored locally on your current device. They won't sync across different devices or browsers.

Is there a limit to how many tasks I can add?

There's no hard limit, but browser local storage has size limitations (usually 5-10MB). For a typical to-do list, you can store hundreds or even thousands of tasks without issues.

How do I organize tasks by priority or category?

This basic version doesn't support categories or priorities. You can add this information in the task text itself (e.g., "[HIGH] Complete project proposal").
