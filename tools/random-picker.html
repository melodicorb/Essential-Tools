About Random Picker Tool

Our Random Picker Tool helps you make unbiased selections from a list of items. Whether you're drawing winners for a contest, selecting team members, or just making a decision, this tool provides a fair and random selection process.

  • Select one or multiple items randomly from your list
  • Choose whether items can be selected multiple times
  • Watch the selection process with a customizable animation
  • Copy or save your results for future reference

How to Use:

  1. Enter your items in the text area, one item per line
  2. Choose how many items you want to select
  3. Select whether items can be picked multiple times
  4. Click "Pick Random Items" to start the selection
  5. View your results and copy or save them if needed

FAQ:

Is the selection truly random?

Yes, our tool uses a cryptographically strong random number generator to ensure fair and unbiased selections.

What's the difference between "With Replacement" and "Without Replacement"?

"With Replacement" means an item can be selected multiple times. "Without Replacement" ensures each item is selected at most once.

Is there a limit to how many items I can enter?

The tool can handle hundreds of items, but for best performance, we recommend keeping your list under 1000 items.

Can I save my list for future use?

Yes, your list will be automatically saved in your browser's local storage. You can also export your results.
