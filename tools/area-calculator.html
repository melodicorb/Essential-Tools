About Area Calculator

The Area Calculator is a versatile tool that allows you to calculate the area of various geometric shapes with precision. Whether you need to find the area of a rectangle, circle, triangle, or other common shapes, this calculator provides accurate results in your preferred unit of measurement.

How to Use

  1. Select the shape you want to calculate the area for from the dropdown menu.
  2. Enter the required dimensions for your selected shape (length, width, radius, etc.).
  3. Choose your preferred units of measurement for both input and output.
  4. Click the "Calculate Area" button or enable "Auto Calculate" for instant results.
  5. View your result and the formula used for the calculation.

Features

  • Calculate areas for multiple geometric shapes
  • Support for various units of measurement (metric and imperial)
  • Automatic unit conversion
  • Adjustable decimal precision
  • Calculation history
  • Copy results with a single click
  • Mobile-friendly interface

FAQ

How do I calculate the area of a circle?

Select "Circle" from the shape dropdown, then enter the radius of the circle. The area will be calculated using the formula: Area = π × r², where r is the radius.

What units can I use for my measurements?

You can input measurements in meters (m), centimeters (cm), millimeters (mm), inches (in), feet (ft), or yards (yd). The output can be displayed in square units (m², cm², etc.) as well as acres or hectares.

How accurate are the calculations?

The calculator provides highly accurate results. You can adjust the decimal precision from 0 to 6 decimal places depending on your needs.

Can I calculate the area of irregular shapes?

This calculator is designed for standard geometric shapes. For irregular shapes, you may need to break them down into standard shapes, calculate each area separately, and then add them together.
